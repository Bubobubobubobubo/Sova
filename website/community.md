# Community

Sova is still in its early stages of development. We are actively seeking feedback and contributions from the community. If you have any questions, suggestions, or would like to contribute to the project, please reach out to us through the following channels:
- **GitHub Issues**: Report bugs or request features on our [GitHub Issues page](https://github.com/Bubobubobubobubo/Sova).
- **Email**: Contact us directly at [this address](mailto:raphael.forment@gmail.com).

## Friends

Sova is developed for the international live coding community and for all the live coding artists around! We are grateful for the support and inspiration from the following communities:
- [TOPLAP](https://toplap.org/): The good old central community for live coding artists and enthusiasts.
- [Cookie Collective](https://cookie.paris/): A collective of live coding artists based in Paris + demoscene.
- [livecoding.fr](https://www.livecoding.fr/): A french hub for live coding musicians.
